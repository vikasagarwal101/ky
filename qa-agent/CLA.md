## Why this exists

`qa-agent` is published under the **GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 (AGPLv3)**.

The project owner also wants the ability to:
- offer a hosted version in the future,
- enter into commercial or enterprise agreements,
- relicense future versions if needed,
- transfer stewardship of the project to a future company entity.

This CLA is meant to preserve that flexibility.
