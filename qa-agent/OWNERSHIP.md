# Ownership Notes

## Current copyright owner

As of now, the intended project owner is:

**Vikas Agarwal**

This is the simplest starting point if you may later:
- form a company,
- offer a hosted version,
- enter into commercial/enterprise agreements,
- or dual-license future versions.

## If a company is formed later

If ownership is later moved into a company, the usual path is:

1. Vikas Agarwal forms or designates the legal entity.
2. Vikas Agarwal signs an IP assignment or copyright assignment agreement transferring the project rights to that entity.
3. Future releases can then be published by that company as the copyright owner or project steward.

## Important practical note

Copies already released under AGPLv3 remain under AGPLv3.

What changes is who owns the rights for:
- future releases,
- commercial licensing,
- hosted service terms,
- and relicensing decisions.

## Why the CLA matters

The project's `CLA.md` is intended to ensure that contributions can continue to be used, relicensed, sublicensed, or transferred to a future company entity without needing to chase every contributor later.

## Not legal advice

This file is a practical project note, not legal advice. For a major launch or formal entity transfer, use a real assignment agreement reviewed by counsel.
